## 🎯 Project Overview

A comprehensive productivity and project governance application for Business Analyst (BA) and Project Management (PM) teams.

### Key Challenges Solved

1. **Scope Drift** - Proactive detection and control of unapproved changes
2. **Financial Visibility** - Real-time cost tracking and profitability monitoring
3. **Documentation Inconsistency** - Standardized templates and workflows
4. **Execution Discipline** - End-to-end governance from research to delivery

### Value Propositions

- ✅ **Standardization** - Consistent documentation across all projects
- 💰 **Financial Control** - 15x minimum profit margin tracking
- 🎯 **Scope Management** - Automated CR detection from calls, emails, messages
- 📊 **Governance** - Mandatory review before client communication
- 🏭 **Multi-Industry** - Banking, Insurance, NBFC, Mutual Funds support

---

## 📂 Project Scope

### 9 Functional Workflow Areas

1. **New Project Workflow** - Discovery → Research → Review → Documentation → Approval → Execution
2. **Existing Project Workflow** - Incremental requirements and change management
3. **Sales & Pre-Sales** - Research-backed proposals and presentations
4. **Industry Coverage** - Domain-specific configurations and templates
5. **Task Allocation & Resource Planning** - Dynamic sprint planning
6. **Project Monitoring & Risk Governance** - Daily/weekly reviews and escalations
7. **Scope Drift & CR Control** - Automated detection and notification (CRITICAL)
8. **Financial Governance** - Cost calculation and profitability alerts
9. **Continuous Follow-Up** - Pending tasks and completion guidance
